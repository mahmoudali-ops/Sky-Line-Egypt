Description for Orange Bay Hurghada Snorkeling Trip

At around 8:30 we will pick you up from your hotel and take you to the pier. On a luxuriously equipped boat we sail to the Orange Bay Island.

During the trip, you will snorkel on beautiful coral reefs. Next, we will take you to Orange Bay Island by small boat.

You will spend about 2 hours on Orange Bay Island. Relax on the beautiful sandy beach or go snorkeling around the island.

We then leave the island and take a boat trip to enjoy lunch, then continue by boat for a second snorkeling trip.

At about 16:00 we return to the pier and the driver will be waiting for us there, who will take us to the hotel.
