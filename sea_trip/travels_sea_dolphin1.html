Dolphin World

Dolphin House Snorkeling and Swimming with Dolphins

The dolphins usually look so cute, jump around happily in the sea, always in a good mood. The dolphin excursion is a great experience and swimming with dolphins is the dream of many vacationers in Hurghada. Memnon Reisen makes it come true.

Discover the mysterious world of the Red Sea! Snorkel and swim with wild dolphins in the open sea in Hurghada.

Lean back and relax on the boat and enjoy the picturesque landscape of the area! Enjoy the unlimited colors and endless shapes of the fish in the magical coral reef!

Description for Dolphin House Snorkeling and Swimming with Dolphins

  • Between 7:00 am and 8:00 am (depending on the location of your hotel) we will pick you up from your hotel and drive to the jetty in an air-conditioned vehicle. You will be greeted by your captain and crew.
  • During the excursion we will stop at three different places and you will have the option to swim or snorkel. Snorkel and see the happy, playful dolphins up close.
  • At around 1 p.m. the crew will serve you a delicious lunch.
  • In the afternoon we will reach the landing stage again, from there you will drive back to your hotel. You will be back at your hotel around 3:30 p.m.
  • The dolphin excursion is one of the most wonderful sea excursions you have ever been on.
